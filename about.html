About PockBox

PockBox is not a direct lender; we provide a service that aims to quickly connect customers with lenders that offer loans that may work for them. Over the years PockBox has built a vast network of short term lenders who are able to provide customers with loans between $200 and $2,500. Many customers use short term loans to help with a wide range of emergency expenses. Unlike traditional personal loan stores, PockBox provides customers with a way to borrow money online without going into a store or waiting in line.

The online loan process is easy with PockBox. Customers visit www.pockbox.com, and fill out our application form. The application process is quick, taking less than five minutes to complete from start to finish. Our lenders do require certain personal information including your social security number, banking information, and so on. To learn how PockBox uses your personal information visit our Privacy Policy

Our application forms are safe and secure using 256bit SSL encryption to submit your sensitive information to our servers.

After an application has been submitted, PockBox will attempt to connect the applicant with a lender almost instantly. If you are approved for a personal loan you can receive the funds to your account in most cases within one business day. PockBox is not the lender, but does the work of connecting you with a lender. PockBox customers should always borrow responsibly. PockBox is not a lender, and cannot answer questions regarding your loan. Please review the terms of your loan before accepting. Review interest rates, and be aware of your due date. Should you have any questions about your loan, please contact your lender directly. If you have any questions regarding our services please visit our frequently asked questions page.
