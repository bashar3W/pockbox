FAQ

What is a personal loan and what can I use it for?

People get personal loans to help with home repairs, unexpected expenses, holiday shopping, bills, and more. Such a loan from one of our lenders can help you get the cash you need! Once you fill out our online form, if a lender is available, you can receive money as fast as next business day.

How do you protect my privacy and personal information?

When transmitting your personal information to our lender network, we ensure the highest safety of your personal information. Using SSL encryption your personal data is scrambled in route to our servers, and decrypted once it arrives at PockBox.

How fast can I get the money?

While approval for a short-term loan can be fast, usually within minutes, you are not likely to be able to get money until the next business day. Think of the process as depositing a check into your bank account, typically, you have to wait at least 1 business day before the check clears and the money is available for you to use from your account. If you are approved for a personal loan by one of the lenders in our network, you can receive the cash for your emergency in as little as 1 business day and resolve your short term cash problem in no time!

Do I have to go into a store or wait in line?

Entering your information on PockBox is easy and private. From the comfort of your home desktop, laptop, smart phone, or tablet computer you can complete the entire process without going into a store and waiting in embarrassing lines. The process is easy and private.

Do I qualify for this personal loan?

In order to qualify for a personal loan via PockBox you must be employed or have a regular source of income, earn at least $800 per month, be 18 years of age or older, and be a US citizen. Please note that filling out our online form does not guarantee approval. Approval decisions will still be at the lenders' discretion.
